pyramid.datasets.load_heartrate¶

pyramid.datasets.load_heartrate()[source][source]¶

Uniform heart-rate data.

A sample of heartrate data borrowed from an MIT database. The sample consists of 150 evenly spaced (0.5 seconds) heartrate measurements.

Returns:

hr : np.ndarray

The heartrate vector.

References

[R33]Goldberger AL, Rigney DR. Nonlinear dynamics at the bedside. In: Glass L, Hunter P, McCulloch A, eds. Theory of Heart: Biomechanics, Biophysics, and Nonlinear Dynamics of Cardiac Function. New York: Springer-Verlag, 1991, pp. 583-605.
